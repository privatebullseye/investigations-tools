Purpose of this page

This page is used by Authorised Representatives and Corporate Authorised Representatives of Titan Securities Pty Ltd to submit advertising, marketing material, and website changes for compliance review prior to publication.

Submitting this form creates a dated entry in Titan Securities’ Advertising Compliance Log. This supports supervision obligations and provides evidence that Titan Securities knows what advertising is being proposed, by whom, and on which platform.

Do not publish advertising before approval. After submitting this form, you must email the advertising material (text, screenshots, artwork, PDFs, or links) to Titan Securities for review.

The following information is captured for supervision purposes: submission time, browser/device details, page URL, and public IP address (where available).
